What is CSP to Me

A Place Where Students Learn to Code

Students will learn how to use many different languages such as Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for both front-end and back-end development. They can organize their assignments and thoughts in Jupyter notebook, which is basically a blog site.

A Place Where Students Collaborate

Collaboration is important in any field, especially in Computer Science where you need to work with many people who may have different ideas and thoughts than your own. Through collaboration the team ensures that there is diversity in the project. Which makes it both better for the user and the team itself.

A Place Where You Learn Life Skills

CSP has many assignments that need to be completed in a week, so time management is essential for this class. By organizing one's schedual they can efficiantly do assignments and complete them quickly. Which allows for people to have more time for other classes and extra things they want to do. Not to mention CSP helps you develop logical thinking and creativity, something that is necessary in every field to succeeded.
